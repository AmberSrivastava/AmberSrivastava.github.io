Amber Srivastava

 

I am currently a graduate student pursuing a PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the Department of Mechanical Science and Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I am advised by Prof. Srinivasa M. Salapaka and my research work lies at the intersection of optimization, learning and controls. I obtained my Bachelors in Mechancial Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur in 2014 and worked at ITC Ltd as an Assistant Manager for an year before starting my graduate studies in 2015.
