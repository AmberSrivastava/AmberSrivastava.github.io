Arun Raman

 

I am currently a graduate student pursuing a PhD in Systems and Entrepreneurial Engineering from the Department of Industrial and Enterprise Systems Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I am advised by Prof. Ramavarapu S. Sreenivas. My current research focuses on Synthesis of Liveness Enforcing Supervisory Policies for Discrete-Event/Discrete-State Systems (DEDS Systems) modeled by Petri-Nets.

I got my Masters in Systems and Control Engineering from IIT Bombay in 2012 where I was advised by Prof. Bijnan Bandyopadhyay . For my Master's thesis I worked on developing Sliding Mode Controllers for Fractional Order Systems. Following my Masters, I worked in GE Oil and Gas as an Edison Engineer till 2014, where I was exposed to real world problems in the area of Gas Turbine and Compressor Modeling, Simulation and Control. Thereafter, I had a short stint as a Research Associate at the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad working with Prof. Debjit Roy in the area of Stochastic Modeling of Service Systems.

I am a big cricket fan, and an aspiring long distance runner.
